# Account Verification (KYC)

## Why verification is required
Identity verification (KYC) is mandatory for all users to comply with global
anti-money-laundering (AML) regulations. Unverified accounts cannot deposit,
trade, or withdraw funds.

## Verification levels
- **Basic (Level 1):** Government-issued ID + facial recognition. Daily
  withdrawal limit: 50,000 USDT equivalent.
- **Advanced (Level 2):** Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement
  issued within the last 3 months). Daily withdrawal limit: 8,000,000 USDT
  equivalent.
- **Corporate:** Business registration documents, ownership structure, and
  director IDs. Reviewed case by case, typically within 5 business days.

## How to complete verification
1. Open the app and go to Profile → Identification.
2. Select your country of residence and document type (passport, national ID,
   or driver's license).
3. Upload clear photos of the document. All four corners must be visible and
   text legible. Screenshots and photocopies are rejected.
4. Complete the face verification prompt in good lighting.

## Common rejection reasons
- Blurry or cropped document photos.
- Name or date of birth mismatch between the document and account details.
- Document expired or expiring within 30 days.
- Using a photo of a screen rather than the physical document.

## Processing time
Most Basic verifications complete within 15 minutes. Advanced verification may
take up to 48 hours during high-volume periods. If your verification has been
pending for more than 48 hours, contact support with your case ID — do not
resubmit, as resubmission restarts the review queue.

## Restricted regions
Services are unavailable to residents of sanctioned jurisdictions. If your
country is restricted, verification will be declined and no appeal is possible.
