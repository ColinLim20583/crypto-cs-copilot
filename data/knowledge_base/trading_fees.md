# Trading Fees and VIP Tiers

## Spot trading fees
The base spot fee is 0.10% maker / 0.10% taker. Fees are charged in the asset
received. Paying fees with the platform token gives a 25% discount when the
discount toggle is enabled in account settings.

## Futures trading fees
USDⓈ-M futures base fee: 0.02% maker / 0.05% taker. COIN-M futures: 0.01%
maker / 0.05% taker. Funding fees are exchanged between longs and shorts every
8 hours and are not platform revenue.

## VIP tiers
VIP levels are assessed daily based on 30-day trading volume and platform
token balance:
- VIP 0: < 1M USD volume — 0.10% / 0.10% spot
- VIP 1: ≥ 1M USD — 0.09% / 0.10%
- VIP 2: ≥ 5M USD — 0.08% / 0.10%
- VIP 3: ≥ 20M USD — 0.042% / 0.06%
- Higher tiers (VIP 4–9) offer maker fees down to 0.011% and taker fees down
  to 0.023% on spot.

## Zero-fee promotions
Selected pairs periodically have zero maker fees. Promotional pairs are listed
on the fee schedule page and can change with 24 hours notice.

## Other fees
- Deposit fees: none for crypto deposits.
- Withdrawal fees: dynamic network fee per asset.
- Convert (instant swap): zero fee; pricing includes the spread.
- Margin borrow interest: hourly, per-asset rates published on the margin page.

## Fee disputes
If you believe you were overcharged, provide the order ID and timestamp.
Support can review execution reports and refund verified discrepancies as
trading-fee rebates within 7 business days.
