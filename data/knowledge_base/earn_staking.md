# Earn, Staking, and Flexible Savings

## Product types
- **Flexible Savings:** Deposit and redeem any time. Interest accrues daily
  and is paid to the spot wallet. Real-time APR varies with market demand.
- **Locked Staking:** Fixed terms of 30, 60, 90, or 120 days with higher APR.
  Early redemption returns principal minus interest already distributed and
  takes 48–72 hours.
- **ETH Staking:** Stake ETH and receive a liquid staking token 1:1 that can
  be traded or used as collateral while earning consensus-layer rewards.

## How rewards are calculated
Rewards accrue from the day after subscription (T+1) and are distributed
daily. APR shown is an estimate based on the previous day's on-chain rewards
and is not guaranteed. Compounding occurs only if auto-subscribe is enabled.

## Redemption
Flexible products redeem instantly up to a daily fast-redemption quota;
amounts above the quota arrive the next day. Locked products redeem at term
end automatically unless auto-renew is enabled.

## Risks
Staking products are not bank deposits and are not covered by deposit
insurance. Principal may be exposed to slashing on some networks; the
platform covers slashing losses for its validators, but on-chain protocol
risk cannot be fully eliminated. APRs are variable and past rates do not
predict future rates.

## Taxes
Reward distributions may be taxable income in your jurisdiction. The platform
does not provide tax advice; export your earning history from Reports →
Transaction History for your tax preparer.
